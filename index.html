Welcome to the math rock society!

This is the place to be if you are into this nerdy ass music genre. Whether you are a guitarist, drummer, bassist or just a listener, we welcome every and all math rock fans. We will discuss our favourite artists and share new music, have jam sessions and even go out to watch gigs!

What is math rock?

No, it does not have anything to do with the mathematics of minerals. It is in fact a musical genre. to be more specific, a sub-genre of progressive rock adjacent to genres like midwest emo. It is called "math rock" due to the fact that a lot of songs within the genre are written with wacky time signatures, like 3/4... or 7/16!
